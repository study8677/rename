# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: **be kind, focus on the work, and don't waste people's time.**

- Discuss code, not people. Disagreements about an approach are normal — disagreements about a contributor's identity are not.
- Assume good intent in others. We're all here because we want better tools.
- If you wouldn't say it to a teammate sitting next to you, don't say it in an issue or PR.
- Harassment, doxxing, sexual content, or sustained personal attacks are out of bounds and will get the offender blocked from the repo.

For anything you'd rather not raise in public, email fanjingwen50@gmail.com.

By participating in this project you agree to these rules.
